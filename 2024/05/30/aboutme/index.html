About Me

徐昕宇 Xinyu Xu

I’m a second-year master student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering (SEIEE), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

I do research in MVIG lab advised by Prof. Yong-Lu Li and Prof. Cewu Lu. I was a research intern in Tencent Robotics-X lab. My research interests include embodied AI, computer vision, and robotics.
